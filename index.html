The Sacred Art of Kolam

Daily Ritual

Every morning, women across South India create intricate Kolam patterns at their doorsteps, welcoming prosperity and warding off evil spirits.

Spiritual Significance

These geometric patterns represent the cosmic order, connecting the earthly realm with divine harmony through sacred geometry.

Generational Wisdom

Mathematical Foundation

Geometry in Motion

Grid Systems

Kolams are constructed on dot grids, using mathematical principles of spacing and proportion.

Symmetry

Perfect rotational and reflective symmetry creates harmonious patterns that please the eye.

Fractals

Self-similar patterns repeat at different scales, embodying fractal geometry principles.
